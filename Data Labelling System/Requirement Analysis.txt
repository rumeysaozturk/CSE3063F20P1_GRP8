REQUIREMENT ANALYSIS
The main subject of the project is labeling the data in the textual data. Labeling  provides the data is more meaningful, informative, summarizer, accesible.

Functional requirements :  (zorunlu olan,kural gibi what behaviours)
1-There are labels and instances in the dataset.
1-Users,Labels,Instances have an ID.
2-Label is assigned on instance.

Non Functional requirements : (zorunlu olmayan olarak anladım,daha çok how it works)
1-There can be many users. 
2-Labels can label many instances.
